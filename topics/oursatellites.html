Future Plans

Göktürk-3 Satellite

Planned for launch in 2025, Göktürk-3 is a synthetic aperture radar (SAR) Earth observation satellite designed to provide high-resolution images under all weather conditions.

Space Weather Application Center

Set to be operational in 2025, this center will monitor and model the effects of space weather changes, enhancing Turkey's capabilities in space.

Moon Mission

Planned lunar landing mission to develop and test national space technologies.
